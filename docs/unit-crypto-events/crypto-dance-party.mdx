# Crypto Dance Party

Unit Crypto Dance Parties are a series of networking events held worldwide, designed to bring together individuals from the cryptocurrency and blockchain community in a fun and engaging atmosphere.

These events provide opportunities for networking, socializing, and building connections, while also promoting Unit, token economy principles, and the wider adoption of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.
